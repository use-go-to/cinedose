Our Concept

Do you know this situation? You open Netflix, Prime, or Canal+, thousands of films are available... and you spend an hour hesitating, doubting, only to end up watching nothing at all.

This is the **paradox of choice** (or "choice overload"), a phenomenon studied by psychologist Barry Schwartz: the more options there are, the harder the decision becomes, and the less satisfaction you feel.

CineDose is the simple solution.
**One single cult film** is proposed every 10 minutes.

No endless scrolling, no regrets. We select a significant work for you, you watch it, and you fully enjoy it.
